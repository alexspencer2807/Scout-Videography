The Development Cycle

How We Develop Athletes

Six steps that turn every match into measurable improvement — continuously, every session.

Step 01
Book & Record

Book a session and we arrive pitchside with the Veo AI camera. Full-match HD footage is captured automatically — no operator needed during play.

Step 02
Receive Your Highlights

Within 24 hours you receive a professionally edited highlight reel — ready to share with coaches, scouts, and academies for scholarship or contract opportunities.

Step 03
Analyse Your Game

Our AI Analyst reviews your footage, tracks your stats across matches, and produces a personalised report pinpointing exactly where to improve — updated every session.

Step 04
VR Training

Rezzil drills on the HTC Vive Pro target the specific weaknesses your analysis revealed. Hundreds of cognitive, technical, and tactical reps — zero physical fatigue.

Step 05
On-Field Practice

Take your sharpened skills back to the pitch. Solidify what you trained in VR with real game reps, live match situations, and competitive play.

Step 06
Repeat & Progress

Each match feeds the next cycle. Your stats, footage, and VR scores are tracked over time — building a complete, measurable picture of your development.

Veo Camera Technology

Powered by Veo

The Veo Camera is one of the world's leading AI-powered sports cameras. It delivers full-pitch automated recording and live streaming without a dedicated camera operator — capturing and broadcasting every moment of your match.

  • AI-powered player tracking — automatic camera movement follows the action
  • Full 180° panoramic pitch coverage — nothing is missed
  • Automatic highlight clip generation — key moments identified by AI
  • HD video delivery within 24 hours — ready to share with coaches and scouts
  • Feeds into AI analytics — your footage becomes data for personalised training plans
Rezzil + HTC Vive Pro

Pro-Level VR Training

Step onto the virtual pitch with the HTC Vive Pro and Rezzil — the exact sport-specific VR training software used by professional clubs worldwide, including Manchester City and Leicester City.

  • HTC Vive Pro — High-fidelity VR headset with millimetre-precision tracking for realistic movement and spatial awareness
  • Rezzil Player — The same VR training software trusted by Manchester City, Leicester City, and other professional clubs worldwide
  • Measurable results — Every drill produces data: reaction time, decision accuracy, peripheral awareness scores
  • Safe & comfortable — Train cognitive skills at match intensity without physical fatigue or injury risk
  • AI integration — Your VR performance data feeds directly into the Scout AI Analyst alongside your Veo match footage

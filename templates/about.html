About Us

Built for Jamaican Athletes

Scout Videography Jamaica is a full-cycle athlete development platform. We combine Veo camera technology for professional match recording, Rezzil VR training on the HTC Vive Pro for cognitive and technical skills development, and AI-powered performance analysis — all in one platform.

Based in Kingston & St. Andrew, serving athletes, clubs, and academies across Jamaica. Our mission: give every serious young footballer access to the same development tools used by professional clubs worldwide.
